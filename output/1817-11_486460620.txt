BURGH REFORMThe Scotsman (1817-1858)BURGH REFORM On Thursday Meeting of the Incorporation of BAKERS tfas held when after great deal of discussion resolutions in favour of reform that is to say of modified reform as may secure to the Incorporation of Bakers the election of thw Deacon wore carried unanimously ANNAN -It must be gratifying to those who are friendly to reform in the Scottish Burghs to hear that the Magistrates and Town-Council have in the most handsome manner and greatly to their credit unanimously agreed to convene the Burgesses and coney wi them in concerting such apian or the future ood management of as the meeting shall approve This example of patriotic virtue and liberality it is to be hoped will be imitated by other burghs
