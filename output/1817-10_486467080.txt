ARBROATHThe Scotsman (1817-1858)OATH At meeting Incorporations of Arbroath on the Gth instant unanimously came to the resolution of petitioning lie Magistrates to admit them and tlie Burgesses to participation in the right of electing thu Town-Council After the alacrity wilh honourable body came forward on occasion when matters of similar import agitated the public feelings it is not supposed that they will discover any aversion to the measure Indeed there is every reason to expect they will act more consistently witli former avowed sentiments refuse to rectify in own system of policy they allow to be so reprehensible in of others
